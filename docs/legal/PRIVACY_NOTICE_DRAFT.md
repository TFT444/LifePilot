# LifePilot Privacy Notice — Draft

**Status:** product/engineering draft for legal review before publication.  
**Market:** United Kingdom.  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-19.

LifePilot is designed to work locally on your Apple device. This notice must be updated with the legal entity name, contact details, final hosting choices, retention periods, subprocessors, lawful bases, and international-transfer safeguards before release.

## What LifePilot processes

| Data | Why | Default location |
|---|---|---|
| Tasks, routines, preferences, corrections | Provide planning and Memory | On device |
| Calendar events and Reminders you permit | Build Timeline, detect conflicts, prepare suggestions | Read from Apple frameworks; LifePilot-owned records stored on device |
| Approximate/current location when permitted | Weather and travel leave-by estimates | Requested only when the feature needs it |
| Approval and execution audit records | Show what was proposed, approved, and executed | On device |
| Optional iCloud sync data | Sync LifePilot-owned records across the user’s Apple devices | Apple CloudKit, only after opt-in |

## What LifePilot does not do in the MVP

- no advertising or cross-app tracking;
- no sale of personal data;
- no banking, payments, shopping, or medical intelligence;
- no Apple Mail ingestion or automatic message sending;
- no autonomous Calendar/Reminders write without explicit approval;
- no mandatory account;
- no remote AI processing by default.

## User controls

Users can:

- decline Calendar, Reminders, Notifications, Location, and Cloud Sync;
- continue using local tasks when permissions are declined;
- turn sensitive notification previews off;
- export LifePilot-owned data;
- delete LifePilot-owned local data;
- inspect and delete Memory items;
- review approval history.
